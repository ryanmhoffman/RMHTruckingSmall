  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    It all started in 2009 with one truck and a dream to begin delivering bulk liquids. After only a few months of working it was time to expand. By 2011, the fleet had grown to 7 trucks.

  • 2012-2015

    Aggressive Growth

    Between 2012 and 2015, RMH Trucking continued to expand, adding new equipment and top notch drivers along the way. During this time our customer base grew too as we earned their trust.

  • 2016-Present

    Rapid Expansion

    In 2016, RMH Trucking opened our second terminal, then third, and now we have 7 terminals as of May 2018. We plan to continue opening new terminals in strategic locations in the coming months.
